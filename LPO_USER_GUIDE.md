## Troubleshooting

### "Clipboard API not supported"
- Your browser may be outdated
- Solution: Use the **Download** options instead

### Nothing happens when I paste
- Make sure you saw the success message first
- Try clicking in the destination before pasting
- Check if your app supports image pasting

### PDF looks different than expected
- This is normal - PDFs are optimized for printing
- The layout is professional and print-ready

### File didn't download
- Check your Downloads folder
- Your browser may have blocked the download
- Look for download notifications in your browser

## Tips & Tricks

1. **For WhatsApp Desktop:**
   - Use "Copy as Image" 
   - Open WhatsApp Web
   - Paste directly into chat (Ctrl+V)

2. **For Email:**
   - Best: "Download as PDF" then attach
   - Alternative: "Copy as Image" and paste into email body

3. **For Printing:**
   - Always use "Download as PDF"
   - PDFs maintain quality when printed

4. **For Reports:**
   - "Download as Image" for PowerPoint presentations
   - "Download as PDF" for formal documents

5. **For Quick Sharing:**
   - "Copy as Image" is fastest
   - No need to save files
